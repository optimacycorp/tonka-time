## 13. Prohibited Uses

- Operating on unsafe slopes, unstable ground, drop-offs, soft shoulders, ice, mud, or unsuitable surfaces.
- Digging without required utility locates, permission, or permits.
- Operating near marked utilities except as allowed by law and safe digging practices.
- Operating under overhead utilities, near energized lines, or near unsafe overhead hazards.
- Using the machine for heavy demolition, concrete breaking, lifting people, trench shoring, or work beyond machine capacity.
- Operating in water, ponds, streams, drainage channels, submerged conditions, or flood conditions.
- Pulling vehicles, large stumps, foundations, structures, or loads beyond the machine capacity.
- Transporting, relocating, subleasing, loaning, modifying, repairing, disabling, or tampering with the equipment without written approval.
- Operating while impaired by alcohol, drugs, medication, fatigue, distraction, or any unsafe condition.
- Continuing to operate after warning lights, overheating, leaks, unusual noises, track issues, instability, impact, or suspected damage.

Any prohibited use is a material breach, may void any damage waiver, and may make Customer fully responsible for all damage, loss, claims, recovery, costs, fees, and injuries.
